Blow Dryers, Curling Irons,and Flat Irons

hair care tips

Heat, in general, is bad for your hair as it opens up your cuticles and dries up any moisture. When concentrated heat is applied to your hair, it becomes dry, damaged, and brittle. Using a curling iron or a flat iron often will leave your hair lifeless and thirsty for moisture. If you do not use your hair dryer the right way, it can also cause damage to your hair shaft

10 Best Water Based Moisturizers For Oily Skin 2018

face care tips

Are you struggling with oily skin? Do not know what skincare products suit your skin type? Not to worry, here are some tips and tricks for treating your oily skin without losing its natural glow and sheen. It is a common misconception that moisturizers are used for treating only dry skin. Moisturizers are even needed by oily skin. They improve skin texture, protect oily skin and mask imperfections. But before using a moisturizer, it is important to understand your skin type. If you are suffering from oily skin, washing away the face every time causes the skin to produce more oil. As a result, the skin looks dull and lifeless. This can add a lot of frustration. Using water based moisturizer for oily skin having acne fighting abilities help reduce the pores, improves skin texture and fights blemishes. A light water based moisturizer can help protect your skin even after washing. Water based products are termed as ‘Noncomedogenic’ which means such moisturizers do not clog the pores.

1. Lotus Herbals Alpha moist Alpha Hydroxy Skin Oil free:

It is an oil-free light product that gets absorbed into the skin easily. It keeps the oil away for at least 6 hours. It keeps away the acne and blemishes and helps give a clear soft and supple skin.

2. Neutrogena Oil Free:

This moisturizer is oil-free and acts as a sunscreen. It gives a matte finish to the skin and keeps the oil away for around 5 hours. It also helps retain the makeup on the skin making it look beautiful.

3. Lacto Calamine Classic:

This is one of the oldest and well known oil-free moisturizer. It also serves as a good make up base and foundation. It helps in controlling acne, blemishes and keeps the skin oil-free for 5-6 hours.

4. Lotus Herbals Skin Whitening White glow Micro Emulsion SPF:

It is a water based moisturizer which is very light.

5. Faces Ever Young 24hr Dual Action Moisturizer:

This is one of the best moisturizers for oily skin. It keeps the skin soft, supple and radiant. It is oil-free and gives a matte look. It keeps your skin oil-free for around 7 hours. It also has a good SPF content, hence, acts as a good sunscreen.

6. Garnier Essential Care:

This is a cheap yet effective moisturizer. It keeps the skin soft and supple giving it a matte finish.

7. Lotus Herbal Acne Gel Tea Tree:

This is a gel based moisturizer and does wonders on your oily skin. This is best for skin suffering from excessive oily skin, acne, pimples and blemishes. It also helps in lightening acne marks.

8. Fabindia Vitamin E Hydrating Cream:

This is a cream based moisturizer and gets absorbed into the skin very quickly. It gives a matte finish and is very light on the skin. It keeps the skin oil free.

9. Clean And Clear Skin Balancing Oil-Free Moisturizers:

This is a light oil-free moisturizer that keeps the skin grease free. It gives a soft, supple and matte finish. It is one of the cheapest moisturizers available in the market.

10. Aroma Magic Almond Moisturizer:

Mix this cream with a few drops of water or aloe vera gel. This combination works excellently on oily skin. It does not clog the pores and makes the skin look radiant.

Reasons to Care for Oily Skin:

Washing face can be a simple solution but it is only temporary. You just can’t get rid of the oil completely. Oily skin needs more care and attention because the pores are large and open. This attracts more dust and dirt and leads to acne breakouts and blemishes. The end result is ugly and lifeless skin. Also, the thick sebum layer that forms on the skin due to excess oil production makes the need for hydration redundant. This makes the skin lack moisture and the pores clogged with oil.

Choosing the Best Water Based Moisturizer for Oily Skin:

The first thing to keep in mind for choosing the best moisturizer is to understand your skin type. Many moisturizers are high in oil content and can make your skin look worse. This will add more problems than solving it. Water based moisturizers are the best for oily skin as they contain very less oil. A water based moisturizer makes the product light and does not clog the pores. It is good to check the oil label on moisturizers. You should always avoid mineral oils in moisturizers. Water based moisturizers with oil control, SPF, hydroxyl acids and glycerin work best for your skin. Now that you know the moisturizer that best suits our skin, it is simpler to choose the one that fits your budget. Just walk into any store, pick one of these moisturizers and take on the world with your clear and glowing skin. And, please do not forget to share your feedback! We would love to hear from you.

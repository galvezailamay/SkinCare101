5 Anti Aging Tips For Body Care

body care tips

Follow our five anti aging tips and give your mature skin the care it needs using the right ingredients:

1. START WITH A SCRUB

Treat yourself to a scrub once a week to remove dead skin cells. You’ll notice your skin will look smoother immediately. Mature skin benefits from being prepared well so that it can better absorb skin care products.

2. USE A RICH, NOURISHING MOISTURISER

Combat dry skin by using a rich moisturiser to nourish and smooth your skin’s appearance. NIVEA’s Firming Body Lotion Q10 Plus ensures that your mature skin is always well-cared for, has plenty of moisture and is toned. The skin-toning formula is enriched with nourishing ingredients: co-enzyme Q10, L-carnitine and creatine, which increase your cells’ energy levels. This strengthens your skin and helps to provide greater elasticity while improving cell renewal.

3. GIVE YOURSELF AN INTENSE MOISTURE BOOST IF NEEDED

If your skin feels particularly dry, try NIVEA’s Q10 Rich Firming Body Lotion. It also contains co-enzyme Q10, L-carnitine and creatine, but in a more concentrated formulation. Your skin will be noticeably firmer after just 2 weeks.

4. CONSIDER BODY CONTOURING

As your skin matures, collagen begins to break down.NIVEA’s Body Contouring Cream Q10 Plus contains co-enzyme Q10 and iso-proteins to boost your skin’s collagen levels for firmer, more toned skin.

5. DON’T FORGET YOUR HANDS

NIVEA Q10 Plus Anti-Age Hand Cream is designed to intensively moisturise your hands and leave them smoother and more youthful in appearance.
